Ever wish you had a 'Build' button in vb6 just like VC, now you can add a build button to your visual basic 6 toolbar. just read this.

Step 1: Right Click on any Toolbar and select Customize

 

Step 2: Select the Commands Tab

Step 3: Select File from Categories list on the left and scroll down until you see Make <Project>...

Step 4: Drag & Drop the Make <Project>... to where you would like to have the Build Toolbar

Step 5: Right Click on the button you have just added and replace the text in the textbox to Build, Right Click Again and select Image and Text

Step 6: Copy this image , now again right click on the build button and select Paste.

Step 7: You have just made/added a build button to vb6 toolbar. you can also edit the image by selecting edit image from the right click menu.
